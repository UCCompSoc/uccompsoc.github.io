We're CompSoc, the club for students studying Computer Science and Software Engineering, and Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Canterbury.

Join us for 2023

One of the biggest and most active societies on campus, CompSoc is proud to have been the principal representative body of tech students studying a highly diverse range of programmes at UC since 2003.

Whether you are studying Computer Science, Software Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics or Data Science, we are here to facilitate you to engage with your departments and colleges, bolster your careers skills, network with our industry partners, and enjoy our range of social events.

What makes us truly special is our range of events.

Our goal is to help our members grow academically, socially and professionally with our wide range of events throughout the year.

From relaxing at an End-of-Term BBQ, challenging yourself and others in our hackathon, to an industry talk with one of our NZ and Australian sponsors, there is always something for everyone.
